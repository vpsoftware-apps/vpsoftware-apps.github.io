🎴 MiniDeck Slides

Privacy Policy  ·  Effective January 1, 2026

The short version: MiniDeck Slides does not collect, store, or share your personal information. Your presentations stay on your device.

Overview

VP Software LLC is committed to protecting your privacy. MiniDeck Slides is designed with privacy in mind — your presentations are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

MiniDeck Slides does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your presentations, slides, or content
  • Your photos or images
  • Your name, email, or personal information
  • Your location
  • Usage analytics or crash data (beyond what Apple collects through iOS)

All presentation data is stored locally on your device using iOS storage.

AI Features (Claude by Anthropic)

When you use the AI deck builder in MiniDeck Slides, the topic or description you type is sent to Anthropic's API servers to generate content. Specifically:

  • Only your typed topic description is sent — not your slides, photos, or other content
  • Anthropic processes this data subject to their Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy
  • Anthropic may use API interactions to improve their models, subject to their policies
  • AI usage count is tracked locally on your device to manage the free trial

Stock Photos (Unsplash)

When you search for stock photos, your search query is sent to Unsplash's API. Unsplash's privacy policy applies to these requests. See unsplash.com/privacy for details. Photo search usage is tracked locally on your device to manage the free trial.

File Sharing

MiniDeck presentations saved as .minideck files contain your slide content and images. When you share these files via AirDrop, email, or other methods, the content becomes subject to the privacy policies of those services. You are responsible for the content you share.

Children's Privacy

MiniDeck Slides is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Apple's Data Collection

Apple may collect certain data through the App Store and iOS platform. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy for details.

Third-Party Services

MiniDeck Slides uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API — AI content generation. Privacy Policy
  • Unsplash API — Stock photo search. Privacy Policy

Data Security

Your presentation data is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS security features. We take reasonable measures to protect the limited data that passes through third-party services.

Changes to This Policy

VP Software LLC may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App Store update notes. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated.

Your Rights

Since MiniDeck Slides does not collect your personal data, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete. Your presentation data exists only on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app or deleting individual presentations within the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or MiniDeck Slides, please contact VP Software LLC through the App Store listing page for MiniDeck Slides.
