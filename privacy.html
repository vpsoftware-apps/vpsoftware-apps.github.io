🎴 MiniDeck Slides Privacy Policy · Effective January 1, 2025 The short version: MiniDeck Slides does not collect, store, or share your personal information. Your presentations stay on your device. Overview VP Software is committed to protecting your privacy. MiniDeck Slides is designed with privacy in mind — your presentations are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. Information We Do NOT Collect MiniDeck Slides does not collect, store, or transmit: Your presentations, slides, or content Your photos or images Your name, email, or personal information Your location Usage analytics or crash data (beyond what Apple collects through iOS) All presentation data is stored locally on your device using iOS storage (UserDefaults and local files). AI Features (Claude by Anthropic) When you use AI features in MiniDeck Slides, the topic or description you type is sent to Anthropic's API servers to generate content. Specifically: Only your typed topic description is sent — not your slides, photos, or other content Anthropic processes this data subject to their Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy Anthropic may use API interactions to improve their models, subject to their policies Your Claude API key (if provided) is stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device only — never transmitted to VP Software A limited free trial is included. Trial usage is tracked locally on your device only Stock Photos (Unsplash) When you search for stock photos, your search query is sent to Unsplash's API. Unsplash's privacy policy applies to these requests — see unsplash.com/privacy for details. Your Unsplash API key (if provided) is stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device only Photo searches are sent to Unsplash — your other app data is not API Keys Any API keys you enter (Claude or Unsplash) are stored exclusively in the iOS Keychain on your device. They are never transmitted to VP Software's servers or any third party other than the respective service (Anthropic or Unsplash) when you use those features. File Sharing MiniDeck presentations saved as .minideck files contain your slide content and images. When you share these files via AirDrop, email, or other methods, the content becomes subject to the privacy policies of those services. You are responsible for the content you share. Children's Privacy MiniDeck Slides is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it. Apple's Data Collection Apple may collect certain data through the App Store and iOS platform, including crash reports and usage statistics if you have opted in to share data with app developers. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy for details. Third-Party Services MiniDeck Slides uses the following third-party services: Anthropic Claude API — AI content generation. Privacy Policy Unsplash API — Stock photo search. Privacy Policy Data Security Your presentation data is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS security features. API keys are stored in the iOS Keychain, which is encrypted and secure. We take reasonable measures to protect the security of the limited data that passes through third-party services. Changes to This Policy VP Software may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App Store update notes. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated. Your Rights Since MiniDeck Slides does not collect your personal data, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete. Your presentation data exists only on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app or deleting individual presentations within the app. Contact Us If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or MiniDeck Slides, please contact VP Software through the App Store listing page for MiniDeck Slides. © 2025 VP Software · MiniDeck Slides · vpsoftware-apps.github.io
